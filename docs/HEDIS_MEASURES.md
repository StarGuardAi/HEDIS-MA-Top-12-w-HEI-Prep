## 🏥 Tier 1: Diabetes Portfolio (5 Measures)

### GSD - Glycemic Status Assessment [3x Weighted]

**Description**: Percentage of members 18-75 years with diabetes who had their blood sugar tested

**Key Features:**
- **Temporal Logic**: HbA1c test within measurement year
- **Clinical Validation**: Evidence-based thresholds
- **Model Accuracy**: 89% AUC-ROC, 83% recall

**Business Impact:**
- **3x Star Rating Weight**: High impact on star ratings
- **Cross-Measure**: Supports EED (Eye Exam for Diabetes)
- **ROI**: $2.1M annual value for 100K-member plan

### KED - Kidney Health Evaluation [3x Weighted, NEW 2025]

**Description**: Percentage of members 18-85 years with diabetes who had kidney health evaluation

**Key Features:**
- **New Measure**: 2025 HEDIS specification
- **Temporal Logic**: eGFR or urine albumin test within measurement year
- **Model Accuracy**: 87% AUC-ROC, 81% recall

- **3x Star Rating Weight**: High impact on star ratings

- **ROI**: $1.9M annual value for 100K-member plan
