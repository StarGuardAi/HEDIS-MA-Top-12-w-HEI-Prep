# Daily SMS Message Suggestions - Family Communication Guide

**Purpose:** Consistent, supportive daily communication with Venise and Ashley during the Spring 2027 relocation journey  
**Frequency:** One brief SMS per day to each family member  
**Tone:** Supportive, informative, calm, forward-looking  
**Timing:** Morning (8-9 AM) or evening (6-7 PM) - consistent time each day

---

## Core Communication Principles

**For Venise:**
- ✅ Extended timeline (Spring 2027, not rushed)
- ✅ Financial security visible ($40-60K annual surplus)
- ✅ Her credit enables Arizona lease (she's essential)
- ✅ Time to say goodbye to Pittsburgh properly

**For Ashley:**
- ✅ 15 months to prepare for move (not 6 months)
- ✅ No pressure for her to change/work/diagnose
- ✅ Family stability maintained (predictable income)
- ✅ Fresh start in Arizona (new environment, less judgment)

**For Both:**
- ✅ This is CHOICE not CRISIS
- ✅ They participate in decisions
- ✅ Strength not desperation

## Message Frequency Guidelines

**Daily:** One SMS per day to each family member (Venise and Ashley)  
**Timing:** Consistent time each day (morning 8-9 AM or evening 6-7 PM)  
**Length:** Brief (1-2 sentences max)  
**Tone:** Supportive, informative, calm

**Exceptions:**
- Skip if Sunday meeting just happened (give space)
- Skip if they've asked for space
- Skip if major family event/celebration (respect the moment)

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ **DON'T:**
- Send multiple messages per day
- Include financial stress or desperation
- Pressure them about the move
- Share major news via SMS (save for Sunday meeting)
- Use guilt or manipulation
- Compare to others' situations
- Include negative emotions or stress

✅ **DO:**
- Keep messages brief and positive
- Focus on progress, not problems
- Emphasize their importance to the plan
- Remind them of extended timeline
- Show financial security
- Express love and support
- Keep it simple and consistent
