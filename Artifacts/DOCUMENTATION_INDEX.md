All three repositories include comprehensive competitive analysis:

1. **Guardian**: [COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md](../project/repo-guardian/docs/COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md)
   - vs FICO Falcon, SAS Fraud Management
   - Cost savings: $300K-$500K annually
   - Performance: 92%+ accuracy vs 88-90%

2. **Foresight**: [COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md](../project/repo-foresight/docs/COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md)
   - vs PredPol, ShotSpotter
   - Cost savings: $200K-$400K annually
   - Performance: 72.5% accuracy vs 68.2%

3. **Cipher**: [COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md](../project/repo-cipher/docs/COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md)
   - vs FireEye, CrowdStrike
   - Cost savings: $400K-$600K annually
   - Performance: 95.3% precision vs 92.8%
